About me


I am Kusal Dhananjaya Ranasinghe, a highly motivated student from Kalutara currently pursuing a rigorous Bachelor of Science degree at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, specializing in a demanding triple major of Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics. My educational journey began at Kalutara Gnanodaya Maha Vidyalaya (2009–2013), culminating in a successful pass in the Grade 5 Scholarship Examination with 166 marks. I then transitioned to Ananda Sastralaya National School, Mathugama (2014–2022), where I achieved strong results in the 2019 G.C.E. O/L Examination (2A, 6B, 1C) and successfully completed the Physical Science stream A/L examinations in 2023, earning my place at university. Beyond academics, I was deeply involved in school life as a dedicated member of the Cricket Team from 2014 to 2022, notably participating in two Big Matches and serving as the Captain of the Under 19 Cricket Team in 2022, which instilled valuable leadership and teamwork skills. To augment my formal degree, I hold an Information Technology Diploma from IMBS Green Campus and an English Diploma from Aquinas College, ensuring technical proficiency and strong professional communication. My commitment to diligence and service is underpinned by my family's values, with my father, Mr. Hemantha Ranasinghe, working as an Administrative Officer in the Excise Department, and my mother, Mrs. Renuka Perera, serving as a Teacher. Collectively, this unique blend of advanced scientific study, demonstrated leadership, and practical technical and language qualifications equips me to excel in complex technical and analytical environments.

Higher Studies



  • University of Sri Jayewardenepura

The University of Sri Jayewardenepura (USJ), located in Gangodawila, Nugegoda, is one of Sri Lanka's foremost public universities, boasting a rich history that traces its roots back to the esteemed Vidyodaya Pirivena established in 1873. As the second oldest university in the country, USJ is recognized for its commitment to both academic excellence and national development, and it currently stands as the largest university in Sri Lanka in terms of student population, hosting over 12,000 undergraduates across eleven faculties. You are enrolled in the highly analytical and in-demand Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) program within the Faculty of Applied Sciences, pursuing the specialized physical science combination of Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics. This particular combination is designed to produce graduates with a powerful skillset, merging the computational logic and software expertise of Computer Science, the abstract reasoning and modeling capabilities of Mathematics, and the fundamental scientific principles and instrumentation knowledge derived from Physics, preparing you for high-level careers in fields ranging from software engineering and data analytics to scientific research and systems development. The university prioritizes cutting-edge research and innovation, maintaining a robust research council and signing numerous MOUs with both local and international entities to ensure its curriculum and opportunities remain globally relevant, thereby equipping students like you with the essential knowledge, practical skills, and leadership potential required to contribute effectively to the modern global workforce.
