About me


I am Kusal Dhananjaya Ranasinghe, a highly motivated student from Kalutara currently pursuing a rigorous Bachelor of Science degree at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, specializing in a demanding triple major of Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics. My educational journey began at Kalutara Gnanodaya Maha Vidyalaya (2009–2013), culminating in a successful pass in the Grade 5 Scholarship Examination with 166 marks. I then transitioned to Ananda Sastralaya National School, Mathugama (2014–2022), where I achieved strong results in the 2019 G.C.E. O/L Examination (2A, 6B, 1C) and successfully completed the Physical Science stream A/L examinations in 2023, earning my place at university. Beyond academics, I was deeply involved in school life as a dedicated member of the Cricket Team from 2014 to 2022, notably participating in two Big Matches and serving as the Captain of the Under 19 Cricket Team in 2022, which instilled valuable leadership and teamwork skills. To augment my formal degree, I hold an Information Technology Diploma from IMBS Green Campus and an English Diploma from Aquinas College, ensuring technical proficiency and strong professional communication. My commitment to diligence and service is underpinned by my family's values, with my father, Mr. Hemantha Ranasinghe, working as an Administrative Officer in the Excise Department, and my mother, Mrs. Renuka Perera, serving as a Teacher. Collectively, this unique blend of advanced scientific study, demonstrated leadership, and practical technical and language qualifications equips me to excel complex technical and analytical environments.
